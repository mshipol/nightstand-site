Features

Everything your reading life needs

Simple to start, powerful over time. Built for how readers actually read — on your phone, before bed, wherever the book takes you.

🔍

Search & scan to add books

Find any book via Google Books or scan the ISBN barcode. Covers, page counts, genres, and series info fill in automatically.

⏱️

Built-in reading timer

Tap Start when you pick up your book, Stop when you put it down. Nightstand logs your time, pages, and progress automatically.

📊

Stats that actually mean something

Streak tracking, yearly goal progress, genre breakdown donut charts, author diversity, and period-based stats — week, month, year, all-time.

🔥

Streaks & habits calendar

Build a reading habit by tracking streaks. The visual heatmap calendar shows every day you read — it's surprisingly motivating.

🏆

48 achievement badges

Earn badges across 10 categories — books completed, pages read, reading hours, streaks, genre firsts, author loyalty, audiobooks, and more. Confetti included.

📚

Beautiful bookshelf view

See your library as a bookshelf with color-extracted spines from book covers — or theme-matched colors for a cohesive aesthetic.

🗂️

Organize your way

Custom shelves, tags, formats (physical, ebook, audiobook), sources (owned, Libby, borrowed, Kindle), and status tracking. Your library, your rules.

🎨

4 beautiful themes

Nightstand (navy/gold), Timeless Classics (warm browns), Autumn Leaves (burnt orange), or Soft Pastels (rose/cream). Light and dark mode with system-default option.

📥

Import from Goodreads

Already on Goodreads? Import your entire reading history via CSV in seconds. Fresh start, zero data loss. Exclude imported books from stats if you want a clean slate.

What makes us different

Not another Goodreads

Nightstand is built for readers who want a beautiful, private, distraction-free way to track their reading life.

Privacy-first

No public profiles, no social feed, no followers. Your reading life is yours. Full account deletion with complete server-side data wipe.

Real achievement variety

48 badges that go beyond "read X books" — genre exploration, author loyalty, midnight reading sessions, 800+ page books. Confetti celebrations and push notifications when earned.

Cover-extracted spine colors

The bookshelf view pulls colors from your actual book covers to create realistic spines. Or choose theme-matched colors for a cohesive library look.

Flexible stats control

Exclude imports or old books from your stats so your metrics reflect your current reading life. Per-book toggle or bulk "start counting from" date picker.

Audiobooks are first-class

Track listening time with the same session timer as physical books. Hours and minutes, pages and progress — audiobooks get equal treatment.

Your data, your way

Import from Goodreads via CSV. Set reading goals. Upload a profile photo. Customize your reading experience — Nightstand adapts to you.
