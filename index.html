The problem

Readers deserve better tools

Goodreads is 20 years old and it shows. Spreadsheets are a chore. Your reading life deserves something better.

😩

Goodreads is broken

Built in 2007 and barely updated since. Clunky UI, no real stats, no habit tracking, no audiobook support. Millions of readers use it because there's been nothing better — until now.

📊

Spreadsheets aren't fun

Some readers track books in Excel. It works, but it's a chore. You shouldn't need a spreadsheet to enjoy reading. You use an app to track your runs — why not your reading?

🧠

You forget what you've read

Without a log, books blur together. That amazing thriller from two years ago — what was it called? Who wrote it? You loved it. You can't remember it. Nightstand fixes that.

🎧

Audiobooks are second-class

Most trackers treat audiobooks like an afterthought. Nightstand tracks your listening time — hours and minutes — with the same start/stop timer as physical reading.

Features

Everything your reading life needs

Simple to start, powerful over time. Built for how readers actually read — on your phone, before bed, wherever the book takes you.

🔍

Search 40 million+ books

Find any book by title, author, or scan the ISBN barcode. Covers, page counts, genres, and series info fill in automatically — no typing required.

⏱️

Just hit Start

Tap Start when you pick up your book, Stop when you put it down. Nightstand logs your session in the background. You don't even have to keep the app open.

📊

Stats that actually mean something

Books read, pages turned, hours listened, streaks held. See your week, month, year, and all-time numbers on a beautiful dashboard.

🔥

Streaks & habits

Build a reading habit by tracking your current and longest streaks. The reading calendar heatmap shows every day you read — it's surprisingly motivating.

🏆

Earn badges

Reading milestones deserve recognition. Earn achievements for finishing your first book, hitting a 7-day streak, reading 1,000 pages in a month, and more.

📥

Import from Goodreads

Already on Goodreads? Import your entire reading history in seconds — every book you've read, rated, or shelved. Fresh start, zero data loss.

📡

Works offline

Log your reading anywhere — on a plane, in the woods, wherever. Nightstand syncs everything back when you're online. Your streaks are always safe.

📱

Phone & tablet, beautifully

Nightstand is built for how readers actually read — on a phone before bed, on a tablet on the couch. The layout adapts to fill every screen size.

🗂️

Organize with shelves

Create custom shelves to organize your library exactly how your brain works — by mood, genre, series, or anything else. Your library, your rules.
